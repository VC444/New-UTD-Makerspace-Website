We'd love to have you!

At UTDesign Makerspace, we believe that everyone has a valuable skill that they can contribute. If you provide your skills, we hope can provide the tools to help you make something awesome whether that be a robot, a duct tape dress, or an amazing community.

Reach out to any one of the officers to express your interest in joining the makerspace as a member, or attend a general meeting to officially sign up to be a part of the makerspace. Don't forget to sign up for our mailing list too!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is UTD makerspace?

it is the place where you can work on your own projects using the tools from makerspace or contribute to existing projects.

Where is it Located

it is located at 3000 Waterview Parkway Richardson, TX 75080 (Next to UTD Design Studio)

Do I need to have any experience for joining this club?

Nope! Not at all! We welcome people from different majors and you may work on the existing project or create your own project.

is There a membership fee?

Nope! it is free. Just come and have fun

What is STM?

STM is Student Toolship Master. As an STM, you can get to access the space, work with tools anytime and also train others to use tools. To become STM, follow instructions by clicking the STM on top navigation bar.

How do I get tools?

Just let one of the officers know and they can help you by writing the proposal.
